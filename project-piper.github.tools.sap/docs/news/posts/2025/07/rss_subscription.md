### 1. Using Browser Extensions

If you prefer to subscribe directly from your browser, you can use an RSS extension. Here‚Äôs how:

=== "Google Chrome"

    1. Install the **[RSS Feed Reader](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/pnjaodmkngahhkoihejjehlcdlnohgmp?utm_source=item-share-cb)** extension from the Chrome Web Store.
    2. After installation, click the RSS Feed Reader icon in your browser toolbar.
    3. Select **"Add Feed"** and paste the RSS feed URL: <https://pages.github.tools.sap/project-piper/feed_rss_created.xml> or just click on "Subscribe" icon, extension will open and just click "Follow".
    4. Click **"Add"** to subscribe. You‚Äôll now see updates directly in RSS Feed Reader.

=== "Mozilla Firefox"

    1. Install the **[Feedbro](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/feedbroreader/)** extension from the Firefox Add-ons store.
    2. Click the Feedbro icon in the toolbar and select **"Add Feed"** or just click on "Subscribe" icon, extension will open and just click "Follow"..
    3. Paste the RSS feed URL: <https://pages.github.tools.sap/project-piper/feed_rss_created.xml>
    4. Save the feed, and you‚Äôll start receiving updates.

=== "Microsoft Edge"

    1. Install the **[RSS Feed Reader](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/feeder-rss-feed-reader)** extension from the Edge Add-ons store.
    2. Open the extension and click **"Add Feed"** or just click on "Subscribe" icon, extension will open and just click "Follow"..
    3. Paste the RSS feed URL and save it to subscribe.
